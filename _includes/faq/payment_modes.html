Can you simply charge to my mobile bill?

You can get the feature and pay for it through your your mobile bill. Look for your mobile operator's name in payment methods. Currently Airtel, Vodafone and Idea support this feature in India.

Mobile carriers charge a convenience fee additionally. This is typically 16%. It may vary by region. More details at https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/6394130.
